How to Use

  1. Position your hand: Place your hand in front of the webcam
  2. Make a gesture: Form a sign language letter (A-Z)
  3. Wait for prediction: The system will recognize and display the letter
  4. Build text: Click "Add" to add predicted letters to your message
  5. Use text: Copy or use text-to-speech for your message

💡 Tips for better recognition:

  • Ensure good lighting on your hand
  • Keep your hand clearly visible in the frame
  • Hold the gesture for a moment before changing
  • Use the landmarks to see how the system see your hand
